Research Methods AI Quiz

Open index.html in a browser to use the quiz.

202 multiple-choice questions generated from the course slides
(L1, L3, L4, L5, Week 2, Week 6). Every question is tagged in its
title with a difficulty level: [EASY], [MID], or [HARD].

Keep these four files in the same folder:
- index.html
- styles.css
- app.js
- questions-data.js

No installation is needed. The quiz runs locally in the browser.
